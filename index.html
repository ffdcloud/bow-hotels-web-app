About Us

A Brief History Of Bow Hotels

Bow Hotels Ltd. began in 1952 as a cozy inn near Alberta's Bow River, founded by local visionary William “Bill” Marston, who wanted to offer travelers a unique blend of comfort and access to Canada's rugged beauty. Over the decades, as word spread of its rustic elegance and warm hospitality, Bow Hotels expanded to nearby cities, evolving into a series of boutique locations celebrated for their inviting atmosphere and natural charm. Today, with locations across Canada's western provinces and the whole world, Bow Hotels Ltd. continues Marstons vision, providing guests with a seamless mix of modern amenities, Canadian heritage, and the spirit of adventure.
